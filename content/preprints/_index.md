The Institute makes its academic preprints openly available, in keeping with the open-science conventions of contemporary philosophy and the cognitive sciences. Each preprint is registered with a Digital Object Identifier (DOI) on Zenodo and is citable as a standalone scholarly work.

Preprints are versions of work intended for, or undergoing, peer-reviewed publication. The published version may differ from the preprint in response to reviewer feedback. Readers are invited to cite the most recent version available; where a final published version exists, it should be preferred.
