The Institute's Working Papers series publishes longer-form research, dialogue documents, and analytical pieces that do not fit the format of journal articles or monographs. Working Papers are made available for examination, citation, and discussion. They do not represent settled positions of the Institute but contributions toward ongoing inquiry.
