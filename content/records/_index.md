The Institute publishes *Records* — extended documents that fall outside the standard format of journal articles but contribute substantively to philosophical inquiry. These include dialogue documents, philosophical correspondence, methodological reports, and field accounts of sustained engagement with AI systems. Each record is made available for examination, citation, and discussion.

Records differ from [Preprints](/preprints/) in form rather than rigour: where preprints are written for the peer-reviewed literature, records are written as standalone documents in their own register.
